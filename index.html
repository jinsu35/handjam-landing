What it is

Pip the fox shows your kid a shape on the iPad. They make the matching hand gesture in front of the camera. Five gestures, five shape friends, ages 4 to 8. No ads, no accounts, no tracking — the camera processes everything on-device.

Race

Free. Beat the clock.

A countdown ticks. Every 5 pairs cleared earns more time. Run as long as you can. Three difficulties; the timer shrinks with each.

Journey

$1.99 one-time unlock.

Follow Pip's journey to wake the stars, told through beautiful illustrations. No clock, no pressure — your kid's pace.

Designed for tiny hands and skeptical grown-ups

  • No ads. Ever.
  • No subscriptions. One $1.99 unlock if you want the full game.
  • No accounts. No login, no email, no profile.
  • No tracking. The camera runs entirely on-device. No frame, image, or audio ever leaves the iPad.
  • Family Sharing. One purchase covers every iPad in the house.

Why I made this

There's a video on X I saw of kids tracing different shapes with each hand at the same time. The exercise builds bilateral coordination, focus, and fine motor control.

I wanted to make something that is just as fun to play and anyone with an iPad can play. Handjam is the result. Pip shows two shapes; you make them with both hands. Hope you enjoy it.
